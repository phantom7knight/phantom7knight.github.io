Hi, I'm Rohit — a game / graphics / tools programmer.

I grew up playing video games; the first one was Dave, back in 2002. That eventually turned into wanting to know how the pixels get on the screen, which turned into a career of putting them there myself. These days I spend most of my time building Fusion, a rendering engine on D3D12 and Vulkan.

I enjoy first-person, action-oriented games, and I'm a passionate supporter of Manchester United.
