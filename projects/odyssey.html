Odyssey — endless runner

C++Custom engineOpenGL GraphicsGameplayDigiPen team game

Odyssey is an endless runner built by a student team at DigiPen (GAM550), on a custom C++ engine. I worked as a graphics and gameplay programmer on it.

graphics_work

  • Microfacet BRDF lighting model.
  • Deferred rendering pipeline.
  • Normal / bump mapping.
  • Environment mapping for reflection and refraction; cube-mapped skybox.
  • Bloom post-processing with Gaussian blur.
  • Custom shader work throughout the pipeline.

gameplay_work

  • Camera behavior for death sequences and slow-motion moments.
  • Death events and their animations.
  • Tutorial level with interactive button prompts, using time manipulation to teach the player.
  • Debug UI with ImGui.
