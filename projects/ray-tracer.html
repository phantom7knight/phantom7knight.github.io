projects/ray_tracer

Ray Tracer

C++Path tracingCPU rendering

A CPU ray tracer written in C++, in the spirit of the classic Ray Tracing in One Weekend series — spheres, materials and a camera, traced one bounce at a time until an image falls out.

It renders progressively: the passes accumulate over time, so the image starts noisy and converges — the 500-pass vs 1500-pass comparisons below show the cleanup. Along the way it picked up textured materials, camera depth of field, and dielectrics with both reflection and refraction.
