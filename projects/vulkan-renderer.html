projects/vulkan_renderer

Vulkan Renderer

VulkanC++GLSLImGui

My first attempt at building a renderer with the Vulkan API, written to understand how the API actually works under the hood — the swapchain, synchronization, descriptor sets, all of it, by hand.

The renderer loads textured models and exposes their settings through an ImGui-based UI. Lighting progressed from a classic Phong model to a microfacet BRDF — the old-vs-updated screenshots below show that jump. The longer-term idea was to grow it into a shader prototyping tool: write GLSL, see the result on a model immediately.
